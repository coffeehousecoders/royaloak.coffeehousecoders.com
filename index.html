Coffee House Coders

Royal Oak Meetup

What

A local gathering of developers that meet to hack on personal projects, talk technology, and shoot the breeze.

When

Every Wednesday night from 7:30pm to 9:30pm. Check out Meetup Group or GetTogether.community for more details.
