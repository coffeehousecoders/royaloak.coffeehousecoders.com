Coffee House Coders

Royal Oak Meetup

What

A local gathering of developers that meet to hack on personal projects, talk technology, and shoot the breeze.

When

Every Wednesday night from 7:00pm to 9:00pm. Check out Meetup Group or GetTogether.community for more details.

Note: We join forces with the Ann Arbor CHC group when they meet. Learn more at http://annarbor.coffeehousecoders.com/

Where


View Larger Map

We meet online at https://8x8.vc/decafbad/coffeehousecoders from 7:00pm - 9:00pm.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact craig@decafbad.net

You can also join us in a couple of IRC channels on Libera.Chat. Our official channel is #coffeehousecoders.

Many of us also hang out in #ubuntu-us-mi, the Ubuntu Michigan IRC channel.

Not in Royal Oak? No problem! Check out our other CHC groups.
