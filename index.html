Coffee House Coders

Royal Oak Meetup

What

A local gathering of developers that meet to hack on personal projects, talk technology, and shoot the breeze.

When

Every Wednesday night from 8pm to 10pm. The last Wednesday of each month we have a longer night from 7pm to 10pm.

Where

We meet in the Peet's Coffee on Woodward Avenue (this location used to be a Caribou Coffee).

Address

31901 Woodward Avenue

Royal Oak, MI 48073

Google Map

Contact

If you have any questions, check out our Google Group!


You can also join us in a couple of IRC channels on Freenode. Our official channel is #coffeehousecoders. Many of us also hang out in #ubuntu-us-mi, the Ubuntu Michigan IRC channel.

Not in Royal Oak? No problem! Check out our other CHC groups.
